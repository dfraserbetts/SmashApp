Current Balance And Tuning State

The current tuning model has three distinct layers:

- Power Tuning: canonical underlying power-cost truth.
- Combat Tuning / Monster Baseline Tuning: non-power monster baseline
  contribution.
- Outcome Normalization: downstream radar interpretation and display.

Anti-drift rule:

- If a problem belongs to the power itself, fix it in Power Tuning.
- If a problem belongs to stripped monster body or non-power baseline, fix it in
  Combat Tuning.
- If a problem belongs to radar display curve, fix it in Outcome Normalization.
- Escalate to code only when the issue is routing law, realization law, or a
  missing implementation seam.

Balance North Star:

- Official balance conclusions require runtime-resolved evidence using
  Balance Environment-authored assets through Combat Lab/runtime resolution.
- Balance Environment is the authored test-data scope, not the simulator.
- Combat Lab/runtime is the rules-resolution engine.
- Synthetic fixtures, spreadsheet estimates, simplified probes, and
  non-runtime calculations are diagnostic only unless explicitly routed through
  the live adapter/runtime path and labelled with full provenance.
- Current sequence: attack string calibration first, defence string calibration
  second, attack/defence offset third, real role asset tuning fourth, and cost
  calibration last. Cost values follow runtime truth.
- Do not tune real role assets before attack and defence strings are calibrated
  unless the Lead Designer explicitly approves an experimental value pass.

Current defensive-pool pricing work should not begin from raw win rate alone.
Layer A deterministic canaries must protect mechanics first, Layer B synthetic
batches are targeted diagnostics, and runtime-resolved real authored scenarios
remain primary table-truth balance evidence.

Official balance conclusions must be based on runtime evidence using Balance
Environment-authored assets. Balance Environment data alone is not enough:
reports must identify assetSource, campaignId/campaignName, adapter or
live-loader path, exact command, commit, dirty/clean state, and any unsupported,
ignored, stripped, or approximated mechanics. Reports that bypass Combat
Lab/runtime resolution, or rely on unsupported adapter coverage for material
mechanics, remain diagnostic only for that scope.

Serious numeric balance tuning is additionally gated by formula architecture.
The current formula work has corrected Monster Outcome Calculator Phase 1
expected output for supported natural/equipped attacks, but packet-level power
expected output still depends on callers supplying packet data, and structured
trait reliability, Forge enforcement, and final scenario evidence remain open.
Balance Environment assets are currently useful for data-path testing but are
not final balance evidence until campaign-scoped runtime runs validate the
current formula/runtime state and report adapter coverage.

The balance-environment-core scenarioMatrix preset exists for tiny
campaign-scoped smoke runs against live-authored Balance Environment assets.
Use it to confirm hydration, action sources, unsupported notes, and fallback
count before running medium baselines.

Roleplay Outcome Contract Family Status

LOCKED: a standard Outcome Contract may contain multiple explicitly authored
variants selected by Intention, Method, Scene Impact, and Scope. Each legal
variant owns its exact Success Outcome, Counter eligibility, examples,
exclusions, and future privilege-cost adjustment. A higher Scene Impact never
generically scales outcome prose; every supported tier must be deliberately
authored. Use one stable family ID when the same recognisable privilege changes
in persistence or scale.

LOCKED: DRAW_HOSTILE_ATTENTION is the first multi-variant family. It is the
Hinder contract named Draw Hostile Attention for Intimidation / Challenge and
One Target, with explicit Minor, Standard, Major, and Legendary variants.
Counter is unavailable at every current tier. The family redirects existing
hostility toward the Ability user; it neither creates hostility nor compels a
hostile act. See the locked rules, costing, builder, and launch-roadmap sources
for the exact variant wording and intrinsic boundaries.


Structured Roleplay Method Status

LOCKED: Specific is removed from the active Roleplay Ability model. Intention
defines the broad lane; Method defines the structured legal approach; Narrative
Theme is required player-authored text explaining how this character performs
the Method; Outcome Contract defines the guaranteed result; Scene Impact and
Scope define approved magnitude/persistence and breadth.

"Method defines the approach. Theme personalises the approach. The Outcome
Contract defines the result."

The standard Method registry contains exactly eight Methods: APPEAL, RALLY,
MISDIRECT, RESCUE, INTERRUPT, CHALLENGE, OVERAWE, and DISCERN_TRUTH. All five Intentions have at least one
approved standard Method. Persuasion owns Appeal and Rally. Appeal maps to the
locked Help / One Target family SECURE_WILLING_COOPERATION, while Rally maps to
the locked Help / Small Group family ESTABLISH_SHARED_RESOLVE, both at all four
Impact tiers. ESTABLISH_SHARED_RESOLVE is the first approved Small Group
contract. Large Group and Faction / Army remain unproven and receive no approved
standard contract in this pass.
Misdirect is the approved Deception Method and maps to the locked Hinder / One
Target family ESTABLISH_FALSE_BELIEF at all four Impact tiers. Misdirect covers
direct falsehood, omission, implication, distraction, selective truth, staged
cues, and deceptive framing; classification follows the materially false
conclusion deliberately created, not whether each literal sentence is false.
Discern Truth has two compatible locked standard Help contract families for One
Target at Minor, Standard, Major, and Legendary Impact, in registry order:
UNCOVER_CONCEALED_TRUTH and REVEAL_EXPLOITABLE_WEAKNESS. Counter is unavailable
for both families at every tier. Uncover Concealed Truth answers what hidden
fact is true. Reveal Exploitable Weakness answers what actionable weakness,
opening, route, pattern, dependency, or leverage point concerning the target
can be used. The selected contract defines the purchased entitlement; the
families are not merged or silently exchanged. The registry therefore contains
ten standard Outcome Contract families and thirty-one exact standard
variants.

No Spot Weakness, Investigate, Search, Analyse, or synonymous Method is added:
Discern Truth already owns observation, deduction, evidence, physical detail,
intuition, contradictions, connected clues, and perceptive insight. Legacy Specific
values may migrate into Methods, but Specific is no longer player-facing or
normalized runtime state. General Custom Outcome review remains available for
requests outside the standard registry and is never silently migrated merely
because a later standard contract exists.

RESCUE remains the Intervention Method for both approved rescue results; no
Extract, Catch, Pull Free, Shield With Body, Stabilise, Escort, Evacuate, or
synonymous Method is added. HIDE_FROM_IMMEDIATE_DANGER remains the unchanged
Minor / One Target concealment privilege. SECURE_IMMEDIATE_SAFETY / Secure
Immediate Safety is the accepted v0 working name, Help, Intervention / Rescue /
Standard / One Target, never Counter eligible, and has exactly one variant:

- STANDARD / SECURE_IMMEDIATE_SAFETY: "the target is secured from one declared
  immediate peril and is no longer directly threatened by it"

Minor, Major, and Legendary variants do not exist. Empty higher-tier cells are
intentional and cannot be filled by generic Impact scaling. A qualifying
Declared Immediate Peril is a concrete, bounded, current or imminent threat to
the selected target, has a recognisable coherent safe state, is reachable
through Rescue and Theme, and fits Standard / One Target. Impossible, overbroad,
inaccessible, or incoherent rescues are rejected before Difficulty.

Success fully establishes safety from the accepted peril without another
movement, defence, rescue, or target-action roll. The GD may introduce a
coherent previously unspecified route or safe detail as Scene Manifestation;
lack of a prewritten route cannot reduce a successful result, while fiction
need not supply an impossible route. Any relocation is narrative, not measured
combat movement, and the player does not select a precise tactical destination.

The safe state remains scene truth until voluntary re-entry, spreading danger,
failed shelter, breached sanctuary, material environmental change, a genuinely
new threat, or another identifiable development defeats it. The source may
remain dangerous. Hide remains Minor concealment; Deny Imminent Hostile Act
remains Major / Interrupt / Counter-eligible and fails an action. Secure
Immediate Safety changes the endangered target's scene state before formal
hostile declaration, cannot be used as a Counter once the Response Window
begins, and replaces no combat response or movement rule.

Declared Immediate Peril, route, destination, and safe position are runtime
only. No peril/safety/target field, numeric cost, cooldown, movement value,
defence mechanic, or combat-runtime behaviour is added.

REVEAL_EXPLOITABLE_WEAKNESS / Reveal Exploitable Weakness is the accepted v0
working name, remains eligible for the naming pass, is Help, Perception /
Discern Truth / One Target, has Minor through Legendary variants, and is never
Counter eligible. Its exact Success Outcomes and reserved privilege-cost keys
are:

- MINOR / REVEAL_EXPLOITABLE_WEAKNESS_MINOR: "you identify one small
  immediately useful weakness, opening, or opportunity concerning the target
  for the current meaningful exchange; if no qualifying exploitable
  opportunity exists, you learn that none is presently accessible"
- STANDARD / REVEAL_EXPLOITABLE_WEAKNESS_STANDARD: "you identify one useful
  exploitable weakness, route, pattern, dependency, or leverage point
  concerning the target for the current situation; if no qualifying
  exploitable opportunity exists, you learn that none is presently accessible"
- MAJOR / REVEAL_EXPLOITABLE_WEAKNESS_MAJOR: "you reveal one central
  exploitable vulnerability or opportunity concerning the target that is
  shaping the current scene and can materially change how it is approached; if
  no qualifying exploitable opportunity exists, you learn that none is
  presently accessible"
- LEGENDARY / REVEAL_EXPLOITABLE_WEAKNESS_LEGENDARY: "you reveal one defining
  vulnerability, hidden route, dependency, or leverage point concerning the
  target whose significance extends beyond the current scene; if no qualifying
  exploitable opportunity exists, you learn that none is presently accessible"

For Roleplay Abilities, target means the bounded subject to which the selected
contract applies. A One Target subject may be a character, creature, object,
mechanism, bounded location or structure, plan, ritual, environmental feature,
or another coherent specifically identified scene subject when the contract and
fiction support it. A nominal target cannot disguise a diffuse crowd, unrelated
subjects, battlefield, city, faction, army, whole war, every organisation
member, or abstract collection and bypass Scope. The GD judges boundary and
access before Attribute or Difficulty.

A qualifying exploitable opportunity concerns the accepted target, directly
answers the accepted runtime Declared Aim, is true or fictionally coherent,
Impact-appropriate, accessible through Discern Truth, Theme, access, and
fiction, actionable rather than trivia, and not already known in the same
actionable form. It may be established, inferred, an existing mechanical
property, or a coherent previously unspecified Scene Manifestation that does
not contradict locked fiction or grant another unpurchased outcome. Lack of a
prepared weakness and plot inconvenience never justify "none"; that fallback
is legal only when no qualifying opportunity is genuinely accessible. The
result informs a meaningful next choice but does not automatically exploit the
opportunity, alter a mechanical vulnerability, grant quantified output, or make
a later action succeed.

Target, Declared Aim, and revealed information are runtime interpretation only.
No target/subject type, investigation subject, declared objective, weakness, or
opportunity field is saved. No numeric cost, cooldown, privilege value, or
restriction discount is introduced by this family.

ESTABLISH_FALSE_BELIEF / Establish False Belief is a working v0 name eligible
for the later naming pass. Its exact Counter-ineligible variants are:

- MINOR / ESTABLISH_FALSE_BELIEF_MINOR: "the target accepts one small and
  immediately plausible false premise as true for the current meaningful
  exchange and treats it as true when making relevant decisions"
- STANDARD / ESTABLISH_FALSE_BELIEF_STANDARD: "the target genuinely accepts one
  plausible false premise relevant to the current situation as true for the
  rest of the current scene and treats it as true when making relevant
  decisions unless meaningful contradictory evidence resolves the belief"
- MAJOR / ESTABLISH_FALSE_BELIEF_MAJOR: "the target genuinely accepts one
  central false premise shaping the current situation as true for the rest of
  the current scene and continues to treat it as true when making relevant
  decisions unless decisive contradictory evidence, direct experience, or
  narrative resolution ends the belief"
- LEGENDARY / ESTABLISH_FALSE_BELIEF_LEGENDARY: "the target genuinely accepts
  one defining false premise whose consequences extend beyond the current scene
  as true and treats it as true when making relevant decisions until it is
  decisively disproved or narratively resolved"

The Declared Aim for this family is one exact bounded false or materially
misleading premise, stated and judged before Difficulty. On success the target
genuinely accepts it and treats it as true in relevant decisions while retaining
independent judgement, priorities, values, and agency. Contradiction must meet
the selected Impact's threshold; unsupported suspicion or plot inconvenience is
insufficient. Declared Premise is runtime terminology only and no premise field,
numeric cost, cooldown, privilege value, or restriction discount is locked.

ESTABLISH_SHARED_RESOLVE / Establish Shared Resolve is a working v0 name
eligible for the later naming pass. It is Help, Persuasion / Rally, Small Group
only, and Counter-ineligible. Its exact variants are:

- MINOR / ESTABLISH_SHARED_RESOLVE_MINOR: "the selected group steadies around
  one simple immediate course and sincerely pursues it through the current
  meaningful exchange despite ordinary hesitation, confusion, or pressure"
- STANDARD / ESTABLISH_SHARED_RESOLVE_STANDARD: "the selected group adopts one
  clear shared course as its immediate priority for the rest of the current
  scene and sincerely pursues it despite meaningful fear, confusion,
  disagreement, or pressure"
- MAJOR / ESTABLISH_SHARED_RESOLVE_MAJOR: "the selected group commits to one
  difficult shared course for the rest of the current scene and sincerely
  pursues it despite serious fear, division, personal cost, or danger unless
  decisive circumstances or narrative resolution make that course no longer
  coherent"
- LEGENDARY / ESTABLISH_SHARED_RESOLVE_LEGENDARY: "the selected group forms one
  defining shared resolve, pledge, or cause whose consequences extend beyond
  the current scene and sincerely upholds it until it is fulfilled or
  narratively resolved"

Small Group is one clearly bounded, scene-local collection whose members share
the immediate situation and can all directly perceive and understand the same
Method expression. Before Difficulty the player identifies membership, states
the exact contract-relevant group result, and explains how Method and Theme
reach everyone; the GD confirms boundary, access, comprehension, shared
situation, every member's eligibility, Method, contract, Impact, Scope, and
restriction before choosing Attribute and Difficulty. Rally retains its one
Declared Shared Course, while Overawe uses one Declared Opposed Course.
Success applies to every accepted member with no per-member roll,
majority interpretation, or hidden partial failure. Membership is fixed and
does not propagate to outsiders, arrivals, replacements, followers, or
intermediaries. Members retain individual turns, tactics, resources, values,
priorities, and judgement. Individual or whole-group Narrative Resolution must
follow genuine fulfilment, impossibility, or identifiable meaningful fictional
change. No numeric headcount, saved course/member fields, numeric costs,
cooldowns, or Scope multipliers are locked.

OVERAWE / Overawe is the second Intimidation Method, inserted after Challenge.
It uses a credible threat, display of power, authority, reputation, certainty,
omen, or consequence to make continued opposition feel too dangerous or costly.
Challenge contests attention, hostility, or rivalry and retains
DRAW_HOSTILE_ATTENTION; Overawe breaks one bounded course of opposition and owns
BREAK_SHARED_RESOLVE. Appeal uses honest shared interest or reason; Misdirect
uses false or misleading conclusions. False threats and fabricated authority
are Misdirect, not Overawe. Overawe creates no quantified Fear, damage, Control,
condition, bonus, movement, obedience, or permanent emotional rewriting.

BREAK_SHARED_RESOLVE / Break Shared Resolve is the accepted v0 working name,
Hinder, Intimidation / Overawe / Small Group, Minor through Legendary, and never
Counter eligible. Exact outcomes and keys:

- MINOR / BREAK_SHARED_RESOLVE_MINOR: "the selected group breaks off one small
  immediate shared course of opposition for the current meaningful exchange;
  each member may choose another coherent response but does not pursue that
  course"
- STANDARD / BREAK_SHARED_RESOLVE_STANDARD: "the selected group abandons one
  clear shared course of opposition for the rest of the current scene; each
  member may choose another coherent response but does not pursue that course"
- MAJOR / BREAK_SHARED_RESOLVE_MAJOR: "the selected group abandons one central
  shared course of opposition for the rest of the current scene and does not
  resume it despite serious pressure, loyalty, personal cost, or command"
- LEGENDARY / BREAK_SHARED_RESOLVE_LEGENDARY: "the selected group adopts an
  enduring refusal to pursue one defining course of opposition whose
  consequences extend beyond the current scene and maintains that refusal until
  it is narratively resolved"

The Declared Opposed Course is one coherent, shared, current or imminent,
understandable, agency-compatible, accessible, Impact-appropriate course stated
before Difficulty. It cannot bundle demands or seek general obedience. Every
accepted member stops that course without per-member resistance, majority
interpretation, hidden exemptions, or semantic/tactical relabelling. Members
retain hostility, individual agency, and choice of retreat, negotiation,
self-protection, unrelated duties, genuinely distinct objectives, movement,
tactics, and resources. Narrative Resolution may apply to the group or one
member only when genuinely supported.

Break Shared Resolve changes intent in a Roleplay Window before formal hostile
action declaration. It is not a Counter, cannot cancel a resolved action or be
introduced after the Response Window starts, and does not replace combat
responses. No Large Group or Faction / Army variant, saved membership/course
field, numeric cost, cooldown, quantified Fear, Control stack, per-member save,
or combat-runtime mechanic is added.
